Our Whitewater Expeditions

1. Los Dientes del Diablo

Class II (Easy Level) | 1/2 Day

Perfect for beginners, families, and children over 8 years old. The Escudo River offers gentle rapids and calm water stretches, ideal for enjoying the scenery and learning the basics of rafting. It is a relaxing and fun experience for everyone.

  • Distance: 10 km
  • Includes: Guide, equipment, and light lunch.
  • Best Season: April to September.

2. Bear Canyon (Intermediate)

Class III+ (Intermediate/Advanced Level) | Full Day

A more challenging adventure that requires prior experience or good physical condition. This trip takes us through exciting rapids and narrow canyons. Feel the adrenaline and the river's strength! Ideal for those looking for a little more excitement.

  • Distance: 18 km
  • Includes: Expert guide, equipment, and full meal at the end.
  • Best Season: May to October.

3. The Forgotten Torrent (Expert)

Class IV+ (Expert Level) | 2 Days (Includes Camping)

Only for experienced rafters and those over 18 years old. This is our most demanding expedition, with drops and technical rapids. Includes one night of camping under the stars on the riverbank. A true test of courage and teamwork.

  • Distance: 30 km
  • Includes: Certified Level IV guides, all equipment, 4 meals, and camping.
  • Best Season: June to August (High water).
